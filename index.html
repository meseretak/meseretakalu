About Me

As a Principal IT Project Quality Assurance at Awash Bank, I leverage my extensive experience in software quality assurance and DevOps to ensure the highest standards of quality and reliability in IT projects. With over 10 years of experience in the field, I have developed a strong proficiency in various tools and technologies, including Git, GitHub, Jenkins, Selenium, JMeter, Docker, Kubernetes, Puppet, Chef, Ansible, and Nagios.

I am also a certified Scrum Master and Agile practitioner, with a proven track record of leading and facilitating agile teams and processes. My expertise in creating test documents, reports, and matrices, as well as my ability to understand requirements, enable me to deliver high-quality results and ensure customer satisfaction. Additionally, I hold certifications in Java and Agile with Atlassian Jira, demonstrating my commitment to continuous learning and professional development.
